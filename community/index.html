About These Communities

Each community has its own character, leadership style, and focus. Some are more structured, others are flat or anarchist in approach. Some focus on the Hebrew Bible, others on mystical Christianity, and some explore broader patterns in faith and life.

What connects these diverse communities is that they are all led by people who have shown their love to me at some point in time.

What works for one person may not work for another. We list these communities because they can be good for people, but we recognize they're not good for everyone.
